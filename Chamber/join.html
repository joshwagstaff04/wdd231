NP Membership Benefits

  • No membership fee for qualifying nonprofit organizations
  • Listing in the chamber online directory
  • Access to community networking events
  • Eligibility for nonprofit-focused training sessions

Bronze Membership Benefits

  • All NP Membership benefits
  • Business listing with logo in directory
  • Invitation to quarterly member mixers
  • Member-only pricing for select chamber events

Silver Membership Benefits

  • All Bronze benefits
  • Featured spotlight placement on the home page rotation
  • Priority booth selection at chamber expos
  • Access to small business training & workshops

Gold Membership Benefits

  • All Silver benefits
  • Premium homepage spotlight positions
  • Exclusive sponsorship opportunities at major events
  • VIP access and recognition at chamber banquets
